Welcome to My Portfolio! I'm a budding cybersecurity professional with a Master of Science in Cybersecurity and Trusted Systems from Indiana University Purdue University Indianapolis. My academic journey has been marked by a deep dive into theoretical knowledge and practical application, encompassing Advanced Network Security, Vulnerability Management, Cloud Security, and Cyber Risk Assessment.

My professional experience has been equally enriching, beginning with my role as an Associate Systems Engineer at Tata Consultancy Services, where I managed and optimized IT operations, performed vulnerability assessments across thousands of assets, and improved authenticated scanning processes. Following this, my internship at OmniSOC at Indiana University allowed me to gain hands-on experience in setting up and managing Elastic Stack components for network log analysis, conducting research on social engineering attacks, and participating in real-world SOC operations and incident response.

Research and projects have been a cornerstone of my career, with my work on vulnerability management automation and malware scanning systems demonstrating my ability to enhance cybersecurity processes. My projects have focused on streamlining vulnerability report generation, automating malware detection workflows, and improving cloud security administration efficiency. I have conducted detailed studies on social engineering attacks and participated in SOC training exercises, including CTF challenges focused on MITRE ATT&CK and Wireshark. My commitment to staying updated with the latest in cybersecurity is reflected in my certifications, such as ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity and IBM Cybersecurity Analyst Professional Certificate.

My technical skills are broad and comprehensive, covering scripting languages like Javascript, Python, Java, and C, to databases such as MySQL, MSSQL, and PostgreSQL, and tools including AWS, Google Cloud Platform, and Splunk. With professional certifications in Cybersecurity and specialized training from CISA, Cisco and IBM, I bring a well-rounded skill set to the table.

I am passionate about leveraging cybersecurity expertise to protect and optimize IT infrastructures. Through this portfolio, I look forward to sharing my projects, experiences, and the journey that has shaped my career in cybersecurity.

In my spare time, I enjoy building PCs, playing cricket, watching anime, and gaming, especially on titles like Rocket League and Valorant. Cooking has become a favorite activity since moving to the U.S. My curiosity extends to various fields, and I am always eager to learn and explore new areas of interest.
